JIRA Service Desk Comment Button Customization

Options

Switch "Comment Internally" and "Share With Customer"

The "Comment Internally" and "Share With Customer" button will switch place and "Comment Internally" will be shown as primary button

Disable "Share With Customer"

Completely removes the button "Share With Customer"

Reload JIRA Service Desk page for the new setting to take effect
